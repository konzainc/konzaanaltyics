KONZA Internal Operational Alerts – Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how KONZA collects, uses, and safeguards information for the KONZA Internal Operational Alerts SMS Program. The Program delivers event‑driven operational notifications to authorized KONZA employees and staff.

Information We Collect

  • Contact Data: Mobile phone number, employee identifier, department or role.
  • Message Data: Timestamp, delivery status, sender/recipient information, and alert content.
  • Consent & Preference Data: Opt‑in/opt‑out status, STOP/HELP keyword interactions.
  • Technical Data: Network metadata, error codes, carrier responses.

How We Use Information

  • Operate the Program and deliver operational alerts.
  • Maintain reliable SMS services and troubleshoot delivery issues.
  • Enforce Konza security, auditing, and incident‑response obligations.
  • Comply with applicable laws and organizational policies.

Legal Basis

KONZA processes SMS Program data based on legitimate operational interests and employee consent obtained through the secure enrollment workflow.

Retention

Contact and opt‑in records are retained during Program participation. Message metadata may be retained for operational audit, troubleshooting, or security monitoring in accordance with KONZA retention policies. Message content is retained only as required for operational purposes.

Sharing & Disclosure

KONZA does not sell SMS Program data. Limited sharing occurs with:

  • SMS carriers and service providers for message delivery.
  • Internal IT/security departments for audit and system performance analysis.
  • Regulatory or law-enforcement authorities when legally required.

Security

KONZA employs administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect Program data, including controlled access, encryption in transit (where supported), monitoring, and compliance with internal security standards.

Your Choices

  • Reply STOP to stop receiving SMS alerts.
  • Reply HELP or email

    Changes to This Policy

    KONZA may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant updates will be communicated through internal channels.
